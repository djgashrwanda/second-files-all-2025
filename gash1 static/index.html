About GS Kiziguro TSS

Our Principles

At GS Kiziguro TSS, we uphold the following core principles that guide our educational practices, ensuring that every student is nurtured into a responsible and ethical individual.

  • Respect for Culture and Tradition: We celebrate the rich cultural heritage of Rwanda and integrate it into our daily learning processes, ensuring that students grow with pride in their roots.
  • Academic Excellence: We are dedicated to providing high-quality education that challenges students academically, encourages critical thinking, and prepares them for success in life and beyond.
  • Unity and Collaboration: We believe that working together is essential to achieving success. We foster teamwork, collaboration, and strong community ties, building a sense of solidarity among students, teachers, and families.
  • Sustainability and Innovation: GS Kiziguro TSS prepares students for the future by promoting sustainable practices and encouraging innovative thinking in a world that is increasingly shaped by technology and global challenges.
  • Service to the Community: We instill a spirit of giving back to society, teaching students the importance of community service and how to make a meaningful impact in their local and global communities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide an inclusive, high-quality education that equips students with the skills, knowledge, and values needed to become innovative leaders and responsible global citizens.

At GS Kiziguro TSS, we focus on nurturing students not only intellectually but also socially and emotionally, providing a balanced education that respects Rwandan culture while preparing them to thrive in a globalized world. We aim to equip students with:

  • Critical Thinking: Encouraging independent thought and the ability to analyze and solve complex problems.
  • Leadership Skills: Cultivating the qualities of leadership and responsibility in all our students, preparing them to take charge in any field they choose.
  • Respect for Diversity: Promoting an inclusive environment where diversity is respected, and different perspectives are valued.
  • Community Engagement: Encouraging active participation in community and social projects that make a difference in the lives of others.
  • Holistic Development: We believe in developing the whole child, focusing on academic growth, emotional intelligence, and ethical values.

Our Values

The core values at GS Kiziguro TSS serve as a foundation for creating a nurturing environment where students feel valued and supported as they grow into responsible citizens and leaders of tomorrow.

  • Respect and Dignity: We foster an environment where mutual respect is paramount, and every individual is treated with kindness and dignity.
  • Perseverance and Hard Work: We teach our students the value of persistence, encouraging them to persevere through challenges and remain dedicated to their goals.
  • Unity and National Pride: We emphasize the importance of *ubumwe* (unity) and national pride, fostering a deep sense of belonging and responsibility towards the nation and its future.
  • Integrity and Accountability: Students are encouraged to act with honesty and take responsibility for their actions, building a reputation based on trustworthiness.
  • Empathy and Compassion: Our school promotes kindness, understanding, and compassion, encouraging students to be mindful of others' needs and to support one another.
  • Social Responsibility: We encourage students to recognize their role in society and take actions that contribute to the betterment of the community and the world at large.

78.6% passed the state exams at the end of high school.

Date: 15/11/2024

Rwanda’s National Examination and School Inspection Authority (NESA) has announced that 78.6% of students who sat for the state exams at the end of high school successfully passed, marking a significant milestone in the country's education system.

Read more
  • Breaking Down the Results Out of the thousands of students who participated in the exams, the pass rate signifies an improvement compared to previous years. This success is attributed to concerted efforts by the Ministry of Education to enhance the quality of teaching, provide adequate learning materials, and train teachers in modern pedagogical practices. Girls' performance was particularly commendable, with many female students emerging among the top performers. This achievement is aligned with Rwanda’s gender equity policies in education, which aim to empower girls and provide equal opportunities for all students. Education Sector Reforms Bearing Fruit Speaking during the announcement of the results, Dr. Bernard Bahati, the Director General of NESA, applauded the students and teachers for their hard work. “These results are a testament to the resilience of our students and the dedication of our teachers. We will continue to invest in education to ensure every student has the tools they need to succeed,” he said. The government’s introduction of smart classrooms, increased access to digital learning platforms, and expansion of technical and vocational education training (TVET) programs are among the reforms credited for the improvement in exam results. Future Prospects for Students Students who excelled in the state exams now have the opportunity to pursue further studies at universities and colleges both in Rwanda and abroad. The government continues to support top-performing students through scholarships and grants, encouraging them to contribute to the country’s socio-economic development in the future. As Rwanda continues to prioritize education as a cornerstone for its Vision 2050 goals, this year’s high school pass rate is a clear indicator of the progress being made. Parents, educators, and students alike are celebrating this milestone, which sets a positive tone for the future of education in the country. This achievement underscores Rwanda's commitment to fostering a skilled, educated, and empowered generation ready to tackle global challenges.
