GS Kiziguro TSS Empowers Youth Through Technical Education for Rwanda’s Future

Friday, 8 September, 2024

GS Kiziguro TSS continues its vital role in Rwanda’s journey of education and development. Since its founding, the school has stood as a beacon of hope, offering quality technical education to students in the Gatsibo District and beyond.

Celebrating Our Graduates' Achievements

This year, the school proudly celebrates the achievements of its graduates, who are now skilled in areas such as electrical engineering, carpentry, ICT, and more. These young professionals are poised to make significant contributions to Rwanda’s growing industries, ensuring that the country continues to rise as a regional leader in technology and innovation.

Promoting Unity and Community Values

GS Kiziguro TSS has not only provided academic excellence but has also promoted reconciliation, unity, and a sense of community, instilling in students the values that Rwanda upholds today. Through events like "Umuganda," where students participate in monthly community service activities, the school has fostered both social responsibility and national pride.
